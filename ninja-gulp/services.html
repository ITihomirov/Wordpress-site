WHY SEO?

Success in SEO is all about where you appear in organic search results compared to competitors, with 94% of search engine users clicking on organic results (Wordstream, 2021). With an SEO strategy custom built with your business goals in mind, you can outsmart the rest of your industry and win rankings on the keywords that count. As you climb up the organic listings, driving more and more qualified leads to your site, your growth will compound month on month, resulting in an ever growing return on investment. Having a stronger organic presence than your competitors is vital for business success.

SEO for Health Brands

With 73% of the health industry's web traffic coming from organic search results, an SEO strategy is vital for businesses looking to succeed in this industry. At Vine Digital, we understand the intricacies and importance of creating SEO strategies for health businesses. We have years of experience navigating TGA compliance, and understand how to navigate as well as Google's health industry guidelines: Expertise Authority Trustworthiness (EAT) and Your Money Your Life (YMYL).
